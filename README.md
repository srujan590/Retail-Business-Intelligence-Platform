# 📊 Retail Business Intelligence Platform

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Retail Business Intelligence Platform** is an end-to-end Data Analytics project developed to analyze retail sales data and generate actionable business insights. This project demonstrates the complete analytics workflow, including data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), SQL analysis, dashboard creation, and business reporting.

---

# 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze retail sales performance.
* Identify top-performing products and categories.
* Discover regional sales trends.
* Evaluate profitability and discount impact.
* Build interactive business dashboards.
* Generate data-driven business recommendations.

# 💡 Key Business Insights

* Technology generated the highest sales.
* Consumer segment contributed the largest revenue.
* West region recorded the highest overall sales.
* High discounts negatively impacted profitability.
* A small number of products generated a significant portion of total revenue.
* Some high-sales products still produced losses due to excessive discounting.
* Shipping times remained consistent across most orders.

---

# 📈 Business Recommendations

* Reduce excessive discounting on low-margin products.
* Increase focus on high-performing product categories.
* Strengthen marketing campaigns in top-performing regions.
* Improve profitability of low-performing categories.
* Maintain inventory for high-demand products.
